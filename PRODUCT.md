# hiy.ai

## Register

Both. `/` and `/{slug}` public twin pages are **brand** surfaces (design IS the product — the twin page is what creators share). `/app` dashboard is **product** (design serves the work).

## Who / What / Why

Creators, coaches, founders, and experts who get the same questions endlessly. hiy turns their content (blog, YouTube, résumé, notes) plus a personality interview into a hosted AI twin at `hiy.ai/{username}` — cited, honest by default, embeddable. Visitors always chat free; creators pay for scale (Stripe deferred).

**Positioning: "the honest twin, at Delphi scale."** Delphi.ai is the category reference for capability and polish; hiy's differentiator is trust made visible — citations on every grounded answer, a designed "I don't know" moment, disclosure on every page.

## Brand personality

Warm · candid · crafted. A well-made linen notebook, not a chrome dashboard; a person who says "I don't know" without flinching.

## Visual identity (committed — identity-preservation applies)

- Warm paper canvas `#f4f1ea`, ink `#1c1b18`, forest green `--accent #1f5648` (trust: citations, product actions), terracotta `--accent-2 #c8613a` (energy: marketing CTAs, highlights).
- Fraunces (display serif) + Inter (UI). Committed pairing; do not swap by reflex.
- Signature elements: the living **orb** (twin presence), the **arch card** (portrait/profile frame, Delphi-caliber hero object), floating **artifact chips** (content stats, visitor questions, citation + "I don't know" moments), soft directional pastel **wash** per page.
- Color strategy: Committed — terracotta carries marketing heroes; green carries trust moments; paper carries everything else.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI-SaaS scaffolding: identical icon-card grids, uppercase tracked eyebrows above every section, hero-metric templates, purple-blue gradients, glassmorphism.
- Confident-clone aesthetics: anything that hides the AI-ness. Disclosure is a design feature, not fine print.
- Delphi itself is a *caliber* reference, not a copy target — steal composition energy, never their layout verbatim or their copy.

## Strategic design principles

1. **The twin page is the product's face.** Every `/{slug}` page must look share-worthy — the arch profile card is the brand's hero object.
2. **Honesty is the imagery.** Citation chips and "I don't know" replies are showcased, styled moments — the thing competitors can't show.
3. **One composed hero beats three card grids.** Single dominant idea per fold.
4. **Creator accent personalization** (their color tints their page wash) — the system bends per creator without breaking the brand.
